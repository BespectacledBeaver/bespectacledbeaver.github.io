history in photos

Photographs serve as portals, transporting us to moments that have shaped our world. Whether it's the hustle and bustle of a forgotten marketplace, the stillness of a bygone landscape or humanity's perseverance to do good, these images weave tales that transcend generations.

introduction to photography

Aspiring photographers embark on an exploration of composition, lighting, and technical wonder, unlocking the keys to transforming ordinary scenes into compelling visual stories. This workshop provides the initial push on your journey toward exemplary photography

Adobe lightroom basics

This introductory course equips photography enthusiasts with essential skills to navigate the powerful tools and features of Adobe Lightroom. Harness the potential of exposure, colour grading, and detail enhancement. Only through mastering image manipulation can you unlock the full potential of your captured moments.
